How To Play

Learn, defend, and survive.

  • Correct answers earn gold and activate tower and ability purchases.
  • Farm Towers boost question rewards for the whole run.
  • Sniper Towers hit hard across nearly the whole map.
  • Starting a wave no longer needs a question prompt.
  • Each new match begins with a map selection.
  • If an enemy reaches the base, you lose HP equal to its remaining health.
